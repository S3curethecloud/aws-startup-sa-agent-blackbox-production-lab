# Founder Meeting Agenda

## Meeting objective

Align the founder, AWS SA, engineering, and business stakeholders on the smallest production path that preserves future enterprise scale.

## Agenda

1. Founder vision recap.
2. First customer and first paid workflow.
3. Roadmap surface review.
4. MVP scope and non-scope.
5. AWS service selection.
6. Cost guardrails.
7. Security boundaries.
8. GenAI safety and evaluation.
9. Production readiness plan.
10. Decision log and next actions.

## Questions to ask the founder

- Which feature causes the customer to say yes?
- Which promise would be dangerous to make too early?
- What data can be synthetic for the first demo?
- What must be real evidence?
- What is the maximum monthly AWS budget for the first 60 days?
- Who approves enforcement capabilities?
- What integration must work first?
- What enterprise review do we need to pass first?

## Decision outputs

- MVP release date.
- Demo persona.
- AWS services approved.
- Budget ceiling.
- Security gates.
- Production readiness owner.
- Interview STAR narrative owner.
