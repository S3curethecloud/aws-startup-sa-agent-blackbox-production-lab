# Working Backwards PRFAQ

## Press release

SecureTheCloud Agent Blackbox helps enterprise teams collect governed AI-agent evidence, score risk, explain decisions with citations, and produce audit-ready packages before risky automation reaches production.

## Customer quote

"We needed proof of what our agents did, why risk was flagged, and which controls approved the response. Agent Blackbox gave us that evidence without forcing us into premature runtime enforcement."

## FAQ

### Who is the customer?

Security, platform, AI governance, GRC, and DevSecOps teams adopting AI agents.

### What problem does it solve first?

It creates trustworthy evidence packages and explanations for AI-agent behavior and policy decisions.

### What is not included in MVP?

Autonomous enforcement, customer production mutation, and unmanaged access to sensitive runtime systems.

### Why will customers trust it?

It separates evidence, recommendation, approval, and enforcement; it stores source evidence; and it requires citations and human approval.

### Why AWS?

AWS provides managed identity, serverless compute, durable storage, event workflows, security controls, cost tooling, and Bedrock for governed GenAI without the startup managing heavy infrastructure.
