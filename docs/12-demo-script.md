# Demo Script

## Demo promise

"Agent Blackbox helps enterprise teams collect governed AI-agent evidence, score risk, explain decisions with citations, and prepare audit-ready packages. The MVP is advisory and evidence-first; enforcement is a later governed phase."

## Demo flow

1. Sign in as custodian.
2. Upload or trigger a synthetic agent event.
3. Show evidence normalized into the event timeline.
4. Show deterministic risk checks.
5. Generate a Trust Intelligence explanation with citations.
6. Show Bedrock Guardrails result metadata.
7. Package the evidence bundle.
8. Human approves or rejects recommendation.
9. Show cost and operational dashboard.
10. Explain enterprise hardening path.

## Demo close

"We intentionally separated evidence, recommendation, approval, and enforcement. That lets a startup move fast without making unsafe production claims."
