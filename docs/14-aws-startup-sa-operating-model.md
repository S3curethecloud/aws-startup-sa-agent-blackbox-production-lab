# AWS Startup Solutions Architect Operating Model

## How the SA helps the founder

The SA is not just designing infrastructure. The SA is the founder's early trusted technical advisor.

## Operating mechanisms

| Mechanism | Cadence | Output |
|---|---|---|
| Founder architecture office hours | Weekly | Decisions, blockers, service guidance. |
| Well-Architected mini-review | Every major release | Risks and remediation backlog. |
| Cost review | Weekly during MVP | Spend trend and budget actions. |
| GenAI safety review | Before customer demos | Prompt/eval/guardrail evidence. |
| Product feedback loop | Monthly | AWS service feedback to internal teams. |
| Community content | Monthly | Reference architecture or founder blog. |

## Leadership-principle behaviors

- Customer Obsession: work backwards from the founder's first customer.
- Bias for Action: ship a safe advisory MVP instead of waiting for a perfect enterprise system.
- Frugality: serverless first, cost alarms first, no premature EKS.
- Earn Trust: be clear about demo versus production claims.
- Dive Deep: inspect data flows, auth boundaries, logs, and cost drivers.
- Are Right, A Lot: disconfirm assumptions with Well-Architected review and tests.
- Deliver Results: produce a demo, not just a diagram.
